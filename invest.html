Learn how to invest in CryptoCurrencies - The complete investment guide.

First off, let us clear a few basics: If you want to invest anything in crypto, it is best to go for Bitcoin. Of course, there are many others available, but Bitcoin is one of the oldest and most trusted ones. It has been the best performing asset for the last 10 years. Period. Many people are still scared to move their fiat money into Bitcoin, but when you look at the facts and educate yourself, there is no reason at all for this scepticism.

Most investors buy coins such as Bitcoin, Litecoin, Ethereum, Ripple, and more and wait until their value rise. Once their market prices rise, they sell at a profit. This investing strategy requires one to identify more stable and volatile assets that can shift in value rapidly, resulting in regular profits. Did you know that Bitcoin can be used for daily shopping, too? Yes, it also has many real-life applications.

In Italy, Bitcoin is a payment method number 3 as this study (please translate the page before reading) reveals! It is only surpassed by Paypal and the Italian prepaid card service PostePay. Bitcoin is bigger than Visa and Mastercard in the country.

Bitcoin and crypto have value on their own, the secure and fast and relatively cheap transaction of money over borders. Try to send, let’s say €5.000,- from the UK to Rwanda. By bank, it will take several days. So you will probably use Western Union or another immigrant’s unfriendly service that charges up to 15%. Now try Bitcoin. It will be there, received on somebody’s wallet, in 10 minutes, the fee is a fraction of the 15% stolen from the poorest in the world.
