Welcome! Here, you will find out all about CryptoCurrencies.

Definition: Cryptocurrencies are digital or virtual money which are not controlled by any central bank of any country. There are various types of cryptocurrencies, like:

  • Bitcoin
  • Litecoin
  • Ethereum
  • Ripple
  • Stellar
  • NEO

Satoshi Nakamoto is the name used by the presumed pseudonymous person or persons who developed bitcoin, authored the bitcoin white paper, and created and deployed bitcoin's original reference implementation.

As part of the implementation, Nakamoto also devised the first blockchain database. Satoshi Nakamoto is the pseudonym who penned the original Bitcoin whitepaper and is the identity credited with inventing Bitcoin itself. While several people have claimed to be Satoshi, the true identity has never been verified nor revealed.

A few people who are suspected of being Satoshi are:

  • Criag Wright
  • Dorian Nakamoto
  • Nick Szabo

In the end, we may never know who created Bitcoin, and helped the world advance.

Cryptocurrencies are based on the system of blockchains. A blockchain, just like it's name, is basically a digital ledger, which stores data in the form of blocks. Each block contains various details of the transaction, like

  • The index of the block (it's block number)
  • A timestamp of when it was created
  • Its transaction details
  • Its proof (basically, like a security code)
  • The hash code (another type of security code) of the previous block.

In 1983 , the American cryptographer, David Chaum conceived an anonymous cryptographic electronic money called e-cash. Later, in 1995, he implemented it through Digicash,an early form of cryptographic electronic payments which required user software in order to withdraw notes from a bank and designate specific encrypted keys before it can be sent to a recipient. This allowed the digital currency to be untraceable by the issuing bank, the government, or any third party.

This is the original incident that spurred up the people's interest in cryptocurencies, and also accelerated research and development. The main reason for this attention was that there was no need for any third party to interfere to complete the transaction, and people now did not have to place a huge trust in banks.

Cryptocurrency gained tracktion during the Great Financial Crisis in 2008 , due to lack of comfort for investors in flat currency. It has many advantages that flat currency cannot offer, like

  • Decentralization
  • No third-party
  • Difficult to counterfeit
  • Identity theft is prevented
  • Portability (All you need for millions of Bitcoins is a mobile phone!)
  • Faster transactions
  • Instant Settlement
